Our Models

Loan Eligibility Predictor

Our loan eligibility model evaluates key factors like income, loan amount, credit history, and property area to predict whether an applicant is eligible for a loan. It provides a quick, clear result: “Eligible” or “Not Eligible,” helping financial institutions make faster, more accurate decisions.

Credit Card Default Predictor

Our credit card default prediction model examines payment history, credit balance, and recent transactions to assess the likelihood of default. It provides a straightforward prediction of whether the user is at risk of defaulting, helping financial institutions take proactive measures.

Purchase Prediction

Our purchase prediction model analyzes user behavior, including browsing history, demographics, and past purchases, to predict the likelihood of a future purchase. This helps businesses anticipate customer buying behavior and tailor marketing strategies more effectively.

Admission Chance Prediction

Our admission chance prediction model evaluates applicant data such as GPA, GRE scores, research experience, and extracurricular achievements to estimate the likelihood of acceptance. It provides a percentage-based probability, helping applicants gauge their chances more effectively.

Ice Cream Revenue Prediction

Our ice cream revenue prediction model uses temperature data to forecast ice cream sales revenue. By analyzing the relationship between temperature and customer demand, it helps businesses optimize stock and sales strategies based on weather patterns.

Diabetes Prediction

Our diabetes prediction model analyzes patient data, including blood glucose level, BMI, age, blood pressure, and insulin levels, to determine the likelihood of diabetes. It provides a clear prediction, helping healthcare providers identify risk and take preventive measures.
